📌 About ATAC

ATAC is a minimalist community token created to explore and demonstrate the full lifecycle of a Polygon asset. It serves as a live educational example for token creation, contract verification, and community building in the Web3 space. Total supply is fixed at 1,000 tokens.

🚀 Vision & Utility

  • Educational Resource: Showcasing how to create, verify, and present a token professionally.
  • Governance Experiment: In the future, holders may participate in polls regarding educational content or community initiatives.
  • Proof-of-Concept: Demonstrating that serious token projects can start with minimal resources but clear communication.

🌐 Community & Roadmap

We are laying the groundwork for a transparent and engaged community. Follow our progress:

  • Q1 2026: Token creation, contract verification, and website launch.
  • Q2 2026: Establishment of social channels (Telegram/Discord) for discussion and governance proposals.
  • Q3 2026: First community vote on educational topics or simple fund allocations (if any).
